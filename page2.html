Who is Sailor Moon?

Sailor Moon is the lead protagonist in the 90s anime (Japanese cartoon). The anime is a development of the original manga (Japanese comics), written by Naoko Takeuchi. Sailor Moon (whose non-superheroine name is Usagi) is a normal adolescent girl who can't quite balance studying, food, games and crushes... until one day, a talking cat informs Usagi she is a Moon Princess, capable of magical powers! What ensues is the power of female friendship, love, support, and adventure.

Q&A Sessions with yours truly!

Dear Sailor Moon,

Lately, I've been feeling like there's too much bad and not enough good in the world. I know it's a general thing, but I feel totally overwhelmed, all the time. What can I do to make myself feel better?

Dear 'too much bad',

I think that you writing in shows just how much good there is in the world already! Although it's easy to feel overwhelmed by all the people, places and things going on around you, you've gotta remember that you're just one person! "Treat others the way you want to be treated!" That's the first and best place to start (although I'm sure you're already doing that!)
